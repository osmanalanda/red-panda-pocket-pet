# Digital Pet — Gamified Red Panda Companion App

## 🐼 What We're Building

A beautiful, gamified digital pet app featuring an adorable Red Panda. You'll care for your pet by feeding it, playing with it, and watching it grow — earning XP, leveling up, and collecting coins along the way.

---

### **Features**

- **Feed your Red Panda** — Tap to feed and reduce hunger, earning XP and coins
- **Play with your pet** — Interact to boost happiness, with fun bounce animations
- **Real-time stat decay** — Hunger, happiness, and cleanliness gradually decrease over time (50% faster decay rate)
- **XP & Leveling system** — Every action earns XP; level up with celebratory confetti effects
- **Coin economy** — Earn coins from interactions, spend them on premium food items
- **Achievement badges** — Unlock milestones as you level up and care for your pet
- **Persistent progress** — Your pet's stats, level, and coins are saved even when the app is closed
- **Pet mood system** — Your Red Panda's expression changes based on its hunger and happiness (includes laughing mood)
- **Smart notifications** — Gentle toast alerts when food runs out, pet needs attention, or needs a bath
- **Mini-game selector** — Choose between Tap Rush and Memory Match games
- **Tap Rush** — Reaction-based tap game with countdown, score tracking, and animated rewards modal
- **Memory Match** — 4x4 card matching game with panda-themed icons, time tracking, and bonus rewards
- **Daily Quests** — 3 rotating daily quests with progress tracking and claimable rewards
- **Inventory system** — Buy items from shop, store in inventory, use them on your pet
- **Evolution stages** — Kiki evolves visually: Baby (Lv1-4), Teen (Lv5-9), Adult (Lv10+)
- **Realistic panda design** — High-end 2D sprite with eye patches, snout, fur textures, paws, and ringed tail
- **Sadness filter** — Visual overlay and tear indicator when stats drop critically low
- **Accessory Overlay System** — Equip/unequip Bow Tie, Crown, Scarf that render on top of the character
- **Shop inventory tab** — Toggle between shop and inventory views; accessories equip/unequip on use
- **Kiki's Room** — Interactive room with Wash, Tickle, and Petting mechanics
- **Wash mechanic** — Drag sponge to scrub Kiki, fills cleanliness bar with bubble effects
- **Tickle mechanic** — Rapid tap to make Kiki laugh with shake animation
- **Petting mechanic** — Long press to pet Kiki with floating heart effects
- **Cleanliness stat** — New stat bar that decays over time, restored by washing in Kiki's Room

### **Design**

- **Soft pastel green background** (#E8F5E9) with subtle gradient depth
- **Vibrant orange action buttons** (#FF8A65) with soft shadow / neumorphic styling
- **Deep grey-blue text** for readability and elegance
- **Animated stat bars** — Smooth, color-coded progress bars for Hunger (warm amber), Happiness (soft pink), and Cleanliness (sky blue)
- **Pulse heartbeat animation** on the pet when happy
- **Bounce animation** when feeding or playing
- **Confetti burst** on level-up moments
- **Card-based UI** with soft rounded corners and subtle inner shadows (neumorphism-inspired)
- **Warm golden room theme** for Kiki's Room screen
- Inspired by Tamagotchi nostalgia meets modern iOS design language

---

### **Screens**

1. **Home Screen** — The main playground showing your Red Panda with animated expressions, stat bars (Hunger, Happiness, Cleanliness), XP progress bar, current level, coin balance, action buttons (Feed, Play, Room, Games), and Daily Quests section
2. **Shop Screen** — Browse and buy food items, toys, and accessories using earned coins; toggle to Inventory tab to view and use owned items; accessories equip/unequip
3. **Achievements Screen** — View unlocked badges and milestones with a visual grid layout
4. **Mini Game Selector** — Choose between Tap Rush and Memory Match with reward previews
5. **Kiki's Room** — Interactive room screen with Wash (drag sponge), Tickle (rapid tap), and Pet (long press) mechanics
